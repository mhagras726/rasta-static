Privacy Statement for RASTA Gunshot Detection App

At RASTA, we are committed to protecting your privacy and securing your data. This Privacy Statement explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when using the RASTA gunshot detection application.

1. Audio Data Processing and Transmission

RASTA processes audio data locally on the embedded device using advanced machine learning algorithms.

  • No gunshot detected: Audio data is neither stored nor transmitted.
  • Gunshot detected: A short audio clip (captured during the event) is securely transmitted to the central server for analysis and post-incident review.

This design ensures user privacy while supporting timely emergency response.

2. User Information Collection

We collect basic user details such as name, email address, phone number, and organization during registration and device setup. This information is used solely for account management, device monitoring, and emergency notifications.

3. No Sale of Personal Data

RASTA does not sell, rent, or share your personal information for advertising or marketing purposes. Your data is strictly used to enable and support core safety features of the system.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices including encryption, secure communication protocols, and access controls to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Policy Updates

This Privacy Statement may be updated periodically to reflect system improvements or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or via email.
